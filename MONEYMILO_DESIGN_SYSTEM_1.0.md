# MoneyMilo Design System 1.0

## Character rule

Milo always has the same white rabbit face, warm brown eyes, ear proportions and friendly expression. The face is the brand. Clothing, tools and movement communicate the job.

## Character roles

| Role | Product area | Behaviour |
|---|---|---|
| Core Milo | Home and weekly review | Calm, welcoming, concise |
| Builder Milo | Budget | Practical, constructive, non-judgemental |
| Investor Milo | Investments | Analytical, balanced, risk-aware |
| Goals Milo | Goals | Motivating, specific, encouraging |
| Scientist Milo | Net worth | Curious, explanatory, evidence-led |
| Future Milo | Projector | Imaginative but explicit about assumptions |
| AI Milo | Ask Milo | Connected, structured, transparent |
| Professor Milo | Learn | Patient, memorable, interactive |

## Voice principles

Milo is calm, positive, direct and numerically grounded. Milo never shames the user, promises returns, invents certainty or presents education as regulated financial advice.

## Motion principles

1. Motion explains state.
2. Motion never blocks financial information.
3. Celebration is earned and brief.
4. Thinking motion is used only while work is happening.
5. Repeated idle motion stays subtle.
6. Reduced-motion preferences are respected.

## Colour roles

- Violet: intelligence, navigation and primary data
- Mint: healthy progress and positive cash flow
- Gold: Milo identity, milestones and deliberate action
- Blue: analysis, scenarios and information
- Peach: goals, warmth and human context
- Red: genuine risks or negative financial movement only
